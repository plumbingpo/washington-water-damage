Water Extraction

Residential Water Extraction Services

When water invades your home — whether from a burst pipe, appliance failure, or storm flooding — professional extraction is the safest and fastest option. Washington Restoration provides expert residential water extraction throughout Washington, handling everything from minor leaks to full-scale flooding. Every extraction includes a thorough damage assessment, industrial-grade water removal, complete moisture mapping, content protection, and detailed documentation for your insurance claim.

Standing water poses a serious risk to your family, home, and belongings. Within hours, water can warp hardwood floors, swell drywall, and begin corroding electrical systems. If left untreated for 24 to 48 hours, mold growth becomes almost inevitable. If you discover standing water in your home — even a small amount — we recommend calling for professional help immediately. Our technicians can assess the extent of the damage and begin extraction before secondary damage sets in.

We also handle water damage caused by hidden leaks that have been ongoing for days or weeks. A slow leak behind a wall or under a floor can cause extensive structural damage before you notice any visible signs. Our technicians use thermal imaging cameras and professional moisture meters to locate hidden moisture pockets and ensure complete drying.

Structural Drying

Moisture Monitoring and Structural Drying

Complete structural drying is the most critical step in preventing long-term damage to your home after water intrusion. Proper drying eliminates hidden moisture from walls, subfloors, and cavities, prevents mold growth, and restores your home's structural integrity. At Washington Restoration, every drying project follows IICRC S500 standards with daily moisture monitoring and documentation.

We deploy a full range of drying equipment for residential properties including commercial dehumidifiers, high-velocity air movers, specialty drying systems for hardwood floors and wall cavities, and thermal imaging for ongoing moisture verification. Each home is assessed individually, and our technicians calibrate the drying plan to match your property's specific materials, layout, and damage severity.

Homeowners are often surprised by how thorough professional drying needs to be. Even after visible water is gone, moisture trapped in wall cavities, under flooring, and in structural framing can fuel mold growth and cause wood rot for weeks. We monitor moisture levels daily and don't consider the job complete until every reading reaches pre-loss standards. Most residential drying projects take 3 to 5 days depending on the extent of damage.

Mold Prevention

Mold Prevention and Remediation for Homes

Mold can begin growing within 24 to 48 hours of water exposure, spreading rapidly through your home and creating serious health risks for your family. Washington Restoration provides professional mold prevention as part of every water damage restoration project, as well as standalone mold remediation for existing growth. Our IICRC-certified technicians follow EPA and IICRC S520 protocols for safe, thorough mold treatment.

We recommend mold prevention treatment as part of any water damage restoration project. Applying antimicrobial agents during the drying process is far more effective and cost-efficient than treating mold growth after it has taken hold. However, if you already have visible mold or suspect hidden mold from a previous water event, we provide comprehensive testing, containment, removal, and post-remediation verification to ensure your home is safe and healthy.

Sewage Cleanup

Sewage Backup Cleanup for Homeowners

Sewage backups are among the most hazardous water damage events a homeowner can face. Category 3 black water contains bacteria, viruses, and other pathogens that pose serious health risks. Washington Restoration technicians are specially trained and equipped to handle sewage cleanup safely, following strict IICRC protocols for contaminated water restoration.

Our sewage cleanup process includes containment of the affected area, safe extraction of contaminated water, removal of unsalvageable materials, thorough antimicrobial treatment, complete structural drying, and post-restoration testing to verify your home is safe. We handle the entire process from start to finish so you and your family can return to a clean, healthy home as quickly as possible.
